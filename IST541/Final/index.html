Deckbuilders are a type of board game that requires players to start with a starter deck, and over the course of the game, build a better one.

Deckbuilder Timeline



Dominion

Dominion was, while not necessarily the first board game to employ some sort of deckbuilding element, the first deckbuilding game to truly be popular. It released in 2008, and was made by DOnald X. Vaccarino. It revolves around a medieval theme, and 2-4 players attempt to gather as many victory points as possible by buying new cards or hindering their fellow players.

Box Cover Art for Dominion



DC Deckbuilder

DC Deck-Building Game is a game by Cryptozoic and was released in 2012. In this game, you play as one of the many characters in DC comic canon, and defeat villains while upgrading your deck to gain victory points. There is also a cooperative mode called Crisis, in which you and up to 4 others work together to defeat villains and solve comic-inpsired emergencies. The game has over 10 expansions, with more on the way.

Box Cover Art for DC Deckbuilder Deck 1



Clank!

Clank is a game for 2-4 players released in 2016 by Renegade Game Studios. In it, you attempt to enter a dungeon to gain treasure and escape with your riches. However, a monster has made its lair in said dungeon, and if you make too much noise you may awaken it.

Box Cover art for Clank!



Star Wars: Deckbuilding Game

Star Wars the Deckbuilding Game released this year (2023) and is anothe of Fantasy Flights Star Wars games. In it, you play as either the Rebellion or the Empire and go head to head to destroy each other's bases. You can buy cards such as Star Destroyers or Luke Skywalker to gain an edge, or deny your opponent access to some cards by fulfilling a bounty on them.
